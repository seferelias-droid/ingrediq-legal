Delete Your IngredIQ Account

IngredIQ (developer: Elias Idris) lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. Here's how.

How to delete your account in the app

  1. Open the IngredIQ app
  2. Go to the Profile tab (bottom right)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Delete my account"
  4. Confirm by tapping "Delete Forever"

Your account and all associated data are permanently removed immediately.

Alternative: request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email seferelias@gmail.com from the email address associated with your account, with the subject line "Delete My Account." We will process your request within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When you delete your account, the following are permanently removed:

  • Your account (name, email, login credentials)
  • Your health profile (age, conditions, allergies, dietary preferences, goals)
  • Your scan history
  • Your saved products
  • Your usage data

Data retention

All personal data is deleted immediately upon account deletion. We do not retain any personal information after deletion, except where required by law. Anonymous, non-identifying diagnostic data may be retained for app-stability purposes.
